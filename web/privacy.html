Rotary Connect — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 July 2026

Rotary Connect is a club-management app used by Rotary and Rotaract clubs for attendance, membership, events, communications, and record-keeping. This policy explains what information the app and its backend collect, why, and how it's handled. It covers the Rotary Connect mobile app and its companion admin dashboard.

Information we collect

What's collected depends on how you use the app — a signed-in club member, a club officer, or a walk-in guest checking in.

CategoryDetails
Member profile Name, role/title, club, phone number, email (optional), date of birth, member number, and a PIN. The PIN is stored as a one-way hash — we never store or can see it in plain text.
Attendance Meeting check-in timestamps tied to your member record.
Guest / visitor check-in Name and phone number entered at check-in, the host member's name, visit type, and date — used to log the visit and send a one-time thank-you text.
Photos Photos uploaded to a club's gallery, along with which member uploaded them.
Club records Meeting minutes, documents, polls and votes, event RSVPs, apologies, project updates, and (for treasurers) dues and ledger entries — all scoped to your own club and visible only to that club's members and officers.
Push notification token A device identifier issued by Firebase Cloud Messaging, used to deliver notifications (e.g. meeting reminders, dues reminders) to your device. Not used for advertising.
Technical / diagnostic data Basic request metadata (endpoint, error type, stack trace) when something goes wrong, kept for up to 30 days to fix bugs. No content of your messages or club records is included.
We do not run ads, and we do not sell or share your data with advertisers or data brokers.

How we use this information

  • To run the app's core features: logging you in, recording attendance, showing your club's events/minutes/gallery, and letting officers manage membership and dues.
  • To send SMS or push notifications you'd expect from a club app — check-in confirmations, birthday wishes, dues reminders, guest thank-you texts.
  • To diagnose crashes and errors so we can fix them.

Who we share it with

We use a small number of service providers to run the app. They process data on our behalf and don't use it for their own purposes:

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google) — delivers push notifications.
  • Cloudflare R2 — stores uploaded gallery photos.
  • An SMS provider — sends check-in, birthday, and reminder texts to the phone number on file.
  • A crash-reporting service, when enabled — receives error details as described above, never your club's private records.

Within a club, member and guest data is visible only to that club's own officers and members through the app — never to other clubs.

Permissions the app requests

  • Camera — to scan a QR code for meeting check-in. No photo or video is captured or stored from this; only the code's content is read.
  • Notifications — to deliver the push notifications described above.

Data retention

Club and member records are kept for as long as your club uses Rotary Connect. Diagnostic error logs are automatically deleted after 30 days. A club officer can remove a member's or guest's record on request (see Contact below).

Your choices

  • You can reset your PIN yourself from the login screen.
  • You can ask your club's officers, or us directly, to correct or delete your personal information.
  • You can decline camera or notification permissions; check-in via QR scan and push reminders simply won't be available.

Children's privacy

Rotary Connect is built for adult Rotary/Rotaract club members and is not directed at children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, we'll update the date at the top of this page.
